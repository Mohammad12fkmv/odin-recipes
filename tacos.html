Tacos

Description

These tacos are filled with seasoned meat and fresh toppings, perfect for a quick and tasty meal.

Ingredients

  • Taco shells
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Taco seasoning
  • Olive oil
  • Sour cream
  • Salsa

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef in a pan with olive oil, garlic, and taco seasoning.
  2. Warm the taco shells in the oven.
  3. Fill the taco shells with cooked meat.
  4. Add toppings: chopped tomato, lettuce, cheese, and onion.
  5. Top with sour cream and salsa before serving.
