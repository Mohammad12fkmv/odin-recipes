Spaghetti

Description

This classic spaghetti recipe is a favorite for its rich tomato sauce and tender noodles.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Basil
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the spaghetti noodles according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, saut√© the garlic and onion in olive oil.
  3. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Mix in the tomato sauce and let it simmer.
  5. Combine the sauce with the cooked spaghetti noodles.
  6. Garnish with basil and parmesan cheese before serving.
